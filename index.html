About

Daniel Ryu

Psychology Student

Oi there, I’m Shrek. I’m just a simple ogre who enjoys the peace and quiet of my swamp… when it’s not being invaded by fairy tale creatures or talking donkeys. I’m not much for fancy things—mud baths, a good roar, and some alone time suit me just fine. I’ve got a bit of a rough exterior (literally and figuratively), but once you get past that, I’ve got a big ol’ ogre heart.

I’m married to Fiona, the most amazing ogress in the world, and we’ve got a beautiful, chaotic little family. Life’s an adventure, and while I didn’t ask for company, I’ve learned a thing or two about friendship, love, and the importance of letting others in… even if they’re incredibly annoying at first. (Lookin’ at you, Donkey.)

If you’re looking for someone to slay your dragons, make you laugh (probably at my expense), or whip up a mean slug stew, you’ve found your ogre. Just… don’t touch my swamp, alright?
